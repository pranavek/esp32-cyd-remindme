RemindMe

A tiny ESP32 desk gadget that reminds you to stretch, drink water, and walk.

Plug in your ESP32-2432S028 Cheap Yellow Display (dual-USB rev.) and click below. The flasher runs entirely in your browser via WebSerial — nothing to install.

⚠ Your browser doesn't support WebSerial. Use Chrome, Edge, or any Chromium-based browser on a desktop. ⚠ This page must be served over HTTPS.

Build: __VERSION__

What you'll see

  1. Click Connect & flash and pick the device's serial port.
  2. Click Install RemindMe. Flashing takes ~60 seconds.
  3. The device reboots and creates a Wi-Fi access point named RemindMe-Setup.
  4. Join that network from your phone — a captive portal opens; pick your home Wi-Fi.
  5. The device reboots, joins, and is reachable at http://remindme.local/.

Two USB ports?

Both ports deliver power; only one carries the USB-serial bridge. If the flasher can't see the device, unplug and plug into the other port.
